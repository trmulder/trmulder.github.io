About

I’m a quantitative researcher in finance working at the intersection of asset pricing and statistical learning. My work centers on factor modeling, grouped heterogeneity, and robust inference. I care about clean data engineering, transparent research design, and faithful economic interpretation. Current projects include replication and extension of grouped-heterogeneity sparse models and building a fully-audited factor pipeline from CRSP/Compustat with quarterly characteristics.
