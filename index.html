About

My name is Twan Mulder, and I am 23 years old. Recently, I completed the MSc in Statistical Science at the University of Oxford. Before that, I obtained a double bachelor's degree in econometrics and economics from Erasmus University Rotterdam. This is a competetive and small program, with only around 100 students admitted each year. The program is part of the Econometric Institute in Rotterdam, which was founded by Jan Tinbergen––first Nobel prize winner in economics––and Henri Theil. A famous alumni is Guido Imbens, who won the Nobel Prize in economics in 2021. I graduated Summa Cum Laude from both programs. I wrote my thesis––titled "Spectral Factor Model for Corporate Bonds: Separating Signal from Noise"––on factor modelling in the corporate bond market, under the supervision of Maria Grith.
